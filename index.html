NRB

Rastra Bank (NRB), the Central Bank of Nepal, was established in 1956 under the Nepal Rastra Bank Act, 1955, to discharge the central banking responsibilities including guiding the development of the embryonic domestic financial sector. Since establishment, there has been significant growth in both the number and the activities of the domestic financial institutions in the country.
